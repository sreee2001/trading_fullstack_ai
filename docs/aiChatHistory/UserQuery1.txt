About the job
BASIC PURPOSE: As a Software Engineer (AI) supporting Musket Corp.’s energy trade floor (specializing in refined products), you will design and develop advanced AI-driven tools that enhance trading performance, automate workflows, and unlock market insights. If you’re passionate about applying AI and software engineering to real-world trading challenges—and thrive in a fast-paced, high-impact environment—we encourage you to apply.

Major Responsibilities

AI-Driven Trading Solutions

Develop, deploy, and maintain AI and machine learning applications that support refined products trading strategies, including market forecasting, arbitrage detection, and price correlation modeling. 
Integrate AI models into trading tools and dashboards to improve decision-making speed, market visibility, and risk assessment. 
Design scalable applications that leverage real-time and historical data to support position management, inventory optimization, and trade execution efficiency. 

Software Development & Systems Integration

Write clean, efficient, and scalable code across the full stack to build production-grade applications used by trading, analytics, and operations teams. 
Collaborate with analytics team to optimize data pipelines, integrate APIs, and enhance system performance. 
Implement solutions that interface with trading platforms, market data providers, and internal analytics environments. 

Innovation & Continuous Improvement

Stay current with advancements in AI, machine learning, and data engineering, identifying opportunities to apply emerging technologies within trading workflows. 
Prototype and test new approaches to automation, data visualization, and predictive modeling that deliver measurable trading value. 
Partner with traders and analysts to translate business requirements into scalable, high-impact technical solutions. 

Other duties assigned as needed.

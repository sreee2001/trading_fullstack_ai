## ğŸ“ Key Concepts

### Forecasts
Predictions of future commodity prices with confidence intervals. Available for 1-day, 7-day, and 30-day horizons.

### Backtesting
Simulate trading strategies on historical data to evaluate performance before risking real capital.

### Models
Machine learning models (ARIMA, Prophet, LSTM) trained on historical data to generate forecasts.

### Analytics
Advanced market analysis including correlations, volatility, seasonality, and anomaly detection.
